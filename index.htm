Yi Han


I am a software engineer and HCI researcher with master’s and PhD degrees in Computer Science from the Georgia Institute of Technology. Between my studies, I worked as a researcher at Disney Imagineering, Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT), and VTT Technical Research Centre of Finland. I also have a bachelor’s degree in Information Management from National Central University in Taiwan. Currently, I am a Research Principle Engineer at Delta Electronics.
